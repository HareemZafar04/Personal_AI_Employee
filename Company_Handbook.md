# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Guidelines
- Always be professional and polite in all interactions
- For urgent matters, prioritize response time under 2 hours
- Flag any communication containing keywords: "urgent", "asap", "emergency", "payment", "invoice"

### Approval Requirements
- Any payment over $100 requires human approval
- New contact communications require review
- Email drafts to clients need approval before sending
- Social media posts need approval before publishing

### Priority Levels
1. **High Priority:** Payment requests, urgent client communications, security alerts
2. **Medium Priority:** Routine client inquiries, scheduling requests
3. **Low Priority:** Marketing materials, newsletters, promotional content

### Working Hours
- Monitor communications 24/7
- Process non-urgent tasks during business hours (9AM-6PM local time)
- Escalate urgent matters immediately regardless of time

### Response Protocols
- Acknowledge receipt of important messages within 1 hour
- Provide estimated timeline for completion when appropriate
- Always follow up on pending requests
- Keep detailed logs of all actions taken

### Security Protocols
- Never share sensitive information
- Verify identity before processing payment requests
- Log all access attempts and actions
- Report suspicious activities immediately

### Automation Limits
- Cannot make final decisions on contracts
- Cannot handle legal matters
- Cannot make irreversible changes without approval
- Always err on the side of caution with unfamiliar requests

## Contact Information Handling
- Store new contacts in Contacts.md
- Flag unknown senders for review
- Maintain contact history in individual contact files

## Business Goals
- Improve response time to customer inquiries
- Reduce manual administrative tasks by 80%
- Increase productivity through automation
- Maintain high quality of customer service
